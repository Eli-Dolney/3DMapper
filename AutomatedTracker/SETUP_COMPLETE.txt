🎬 PIPELINE FLOW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Video Input
    ↓
  [1] Frame Extraction (FFmpeg)
    ↓
  [2] Feature Detection (COLMAP SIFT)
    ↓
  [3] Feature Matching (COLMAP)
    ↓
  [4] Structure from Motion (COLMAP Mapper)
    ↓
  [5] Dense Reconstruction (Patch Match + Poisson Meshing)
    ↓
  Output: Cameras.bin, points3D.bin, dense mesh (PLY)
    ↓
  Blender Import + VFX Composition
